K-POP Galaxy

Site Name: K-POP GALAXY

"K-Pop Galaxy" captures the vast, colorful, and ever-expanding world of K-pop music and culture.

Purpose of the Site

The purpose of K-Pop Galaxy is to introduce and celebrate the world of K-pop by focusing on two of its most iconic groups: BTS and Stray Kids. The site will provide detailed information about each member, highlight their most famous songs, and offer insights into their music and achievements.

Also, K-Pop Galaxy will feature a special section dedicated to new and emerging K-pop artists, giving fans the chance to discover fresh talent and expand their musical horizons. The goal is to create an engaging and informative space where fans—both new and longtime—can learn, explore, and connect with the ever-growing universe of K-pop.

Scenarios

  • "I've never listen any music from BTS or Stray Kids before. Where should I start?"
  • "What are the new generation of k-pop music?"
  • "How many members do these k-pop bands have?"
